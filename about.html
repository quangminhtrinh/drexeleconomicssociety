About Us - Drexel Economics Society

Welcome to the Drexel Economics Society, a dynamic community dedicated to fostering a deeper understanding of economics in the modern world. Established by a passionate group of students, our club is a platform where future economists and policymakers converge to explore the ever-evolving landscape of economic theory and practice.

To empower Drexel students with a robust understanding of economic principles and organize events where students can network. We aim to provide a space where ideas are shared, debates are encouraged, and knowledge is expanded through collaboration and engagement.

Weekly Meetings: Engage in vibrant discussions on a range of economic topics, from classic theories to contemporary global economic trends.

Guest Lectures and Panels: Host industry experts, Drexel faculty, and alumni to share insights and experiences, offering members a broader perspective on economic applications.

Workshops and Seminars: Conduct skill-building sessions in areas like economic research methods, data analysis, and financial literacy.

Networking Events: Connect with professionals, alumni, and peers to build relationships and explore career opportunities in economics-related fields.

Community Outreach: Participate in community service projects, emphasizing the application of economic principles for social good.

Our Members: The club is open to all Drexel students, regardless of major, who share an interest in economics. Our diverse membership includes students from economics, business, engineering, and other disciplines, all bringing unique perspectives to our discussions.

Join Us: Whether you are an economics major, a business enthusiast, or simply curious about how economic forces shape our world, Drexel Economics Society is the place for you. Join us to expand your understanding, engage in thought-provoking discussions, and connect with like-minded individuals.
