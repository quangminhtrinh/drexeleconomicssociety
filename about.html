About Us - Drexel Economics Society

The Drexel Economics Society seeks to broaden the understanding of economic fundamentals amongst students of all disciplines through educational, professional, and community-building events and with our presence on campus. It strives to encourage people to explore the various paths available within Economics as a career choice and aims to educate the University population about Economics as an emerging option of study. DES also serves as the bridge connecting students to department faculty, promoting research opportunities, and helping students prepare for Graduate School. Whether you're majoring in Economics or just interested in the facets of Economics that appear in your everyday life, DES invites you to join our community to maximize your marginal utility during your time at Drexel!

To stay updated about all future events, please join our Discord server and/or follow our Instagram.
