DES What's Up: Black History in Economical Perspective

As we enter the month of February, we are happy to celebrate the Black History Month. For this reason, we would be more than happy to see you join us to learn more about the economical perspective of Black History. See you there!

What's Up is designed to provide you the most relevant information you'll need to understand the issue, and provide a space to for you to ask questions and voice your own opinion on the topic! Our goal is to have an event where you can drop by for a short stay and leave with a little more knowledge than you had just 30 minutes prior.
